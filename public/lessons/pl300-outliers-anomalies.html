Detect Outliers and Anomalies

Outlier and anomaly detection identifies unusual data points that may indicate errors, fraud, or significant business events. Statistical methods, visual techniques, and AI tools help surface these important signals—enabling better data quality and decision-making.

Definition:

✓ Unusual magnitude

Detection Methods

📊 Statistical Methods▼

Standard Deviation:

✓ Values ±3σ = outliers
✓ Works for normal dist

IQR Method:

✓ Q1 - 1.5×IQR to Q3 + 1.5×IQR
✓ Better for skewed data

Z-Score:

✓ (Value - Mean) / StdDev
✓ Z > 3 or < -3 = outlier
🎨 Visual Detection▼
