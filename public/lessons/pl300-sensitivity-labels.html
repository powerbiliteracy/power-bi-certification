Apply Sensitivity Labels

Sensitivity labels classify and protect Power BI content based on data sensitivity. Apply labels to reports, dashboards, and datasets to enforce access restrictions, enable encryption, prevent exports, and ensure compliance—automatically protecting sensitive data across your organization.

Scenario 1: Financial Reports

Requirement:

Quarterly financial reports
CFO and controllers only
No export allowed

Solution:

Apply "Highly Confidential" label
Block exports in settings
Restrict to finance group
Scenario 2: Marketing Content

Requirement:

Public-facing reports
No restrictions
Can be shared widely

Solution:

Apply "Public" label
No special protections
Allow all sharing
Scenario 3: Employee Data

Requirement:

HR metrics and data
Internal company only

Solution:

Apply "Internal" label
Block external sharing

Requirement:

Research and development
Specific teams only

Solution:

Apply "Confidential" label
Restrict by group
Limited export capability
